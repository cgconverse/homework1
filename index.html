How to Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Recipe:

  1. Put 2 pieces of bread on a plate.
  2. Spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  3. Spread the jelly on the other slice of bread.
  4. Put the two slices of bread together and cut in half.
  5. Enjoy!
